--- title: "Structural Stability Analysis of Mechanical Systems" permalink: /research/structural-analysis/ author_profile: true ---

I worked on the analysis and design of pressure equipment and thin-walled mechanical systems using finite element analysis (FEA). My responsibilities included optimization design in accordance with Chinese Standard JB/T 4732, ASME BPVC Section VIII, and other relevant codes. I evaluated stress intensity under pressure and thermal loading, optimized structural designs based on FEA results, and performed fatigue and safety assessments through numerical simulation.
